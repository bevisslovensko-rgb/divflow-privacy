Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

DivFlow is a simple offline dividend calculator. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data you enter into the app. All calculations happen locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

DivFlow does not collect any personally identifiable information. The app works completely offline and all investment data you enter (initial investment, dividend yield, etc.) stays on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

DivFlow displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect and use certain data to show relevant ads. This includes:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, operating system, and language
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

This data is collected by Google, not by us. For more information about how Google uses data, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob Privacy Policy

3. User Consent (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), including Slovakia and the Czech Republic, you will be asked to provide consent for personalized advertising when you first launch the app. You can manage your advertising preferences at any time through your device settings.

To opt out of personalized ads on Android:

  • Go to Settings → Google → Ads
  • Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

4. Data Storage

DivFlow does not maintain any databases or servers. No investment data, personal information, or usage statistics are stored outside of your device. The app does not require any account registration or login.

5. Children's Privacy

DivFlow is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

6. Third-Party Services

DivFlow uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
